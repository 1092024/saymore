Introduction

Expressing emotions through art therapy, visualizing the emotions of the experiencer with ink strokes.
Allowing the experiencer to visualize their emotions on rice paper while releasing them, stimulating the brain, and helping to organize emotions.
Converting speech into text for emotional analysis, presenting corresponding animations based on the results, projected onto rice paper.
Assisting the experiencer in understanding and expressing emotions, enhancing mental health.
